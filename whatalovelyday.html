Storyline

Tommy hadn't planned to hide in a trunk to escape his classmates, Harry and Irene hadn't planned to be taken hostage on the road by a bank robber and Ted hadn't planned to be wanted by the police after his theft. What a lovely day, if only they weren't all in the same car.
